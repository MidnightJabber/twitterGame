Selection

  • Click on any card to select it
  • Clicking outside a card will de-select the selected card
  • Beware, If you click on a Celeb first and then on a Tweet or vice-versa, it will count as a pair selection.
  • There is no restriction on click direction. You can click a Celeb first and then a tweet or a Tweet First and then a Celeb

Correct Selection

  • Selecting two cards from different columns consecutively will count as a pair selection
  • If the selection is correct, the highlighted pair will gain a green border signalling that these have been matched correctly
  • For selecting a correct pair you will gain some points. Duh!
  • These points are based on the time you have left/used and the number of correct matches till now
  • This means, a correct selection at 118 seconds will gain alot more points than one at 90 seconds
  • Once a correct pair has been matched, these cards are not clickable/selectable in any manner

Incorrect Selection

  • If the selection is incorrect, the highlighted pair will gain a red border and shake vigourously, signalling that these have been matched incorrectly
  • For selecting an incorrect pair There is a penalty!
  • For every incorrect selection, you lose 10 seconds
  • Be careful, you score is dependant on the time you have left and therefore technically, every incorrect selection loses you potential points.
